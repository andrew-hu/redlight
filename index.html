About Red Light Etcher

This application allows you to take a picture of anything, and then convert the image into black and white, and then laser etch it onto a material of yout choice.

No fuss, no hassel, easy to use, and best of all, you can do it from anywhere.

How it works

A laser etcher is a device that can laser engrave patterns, symbols, markings ect. into a material such as wood, leather, or metal. The settings of the laser can be set in order to get the precise cutsuch as adjusting the dept or size of the laser. After that, the laser then proceeds to imprint the convereted image into the material with a live feedback of the progress.

Questions and Answers

Question:Will I have to have a material already in the machine to start the print?
Answer: Yes, the etcher will not be able to read the material if there is none. Therefore you have to hae the material in the etcher before you try to print.
Question: Lets say halfway through I change my mind and want to cancel the print, is this possible?
Answer:Yes, there will be a kill button, so lets say you mess up or decide to change your mind, you can cancel the print. There will also be a pause button to pause the print if you like aswell.
Question:How long will each print take to print?
Answer: The time it takes to print something factors on many things. For example, if you used metal instead of wood, it would take longer. Also the setting of the laser can vary the print time. If you give the laser more power, it will print faster. In addition, the coplexity of the logo can also effect the print time. All in all, there is no exact time that all prints will take.
Question:Will there be a progress bar or any other way to tell the progress of the print and how close it is to being completed?
Answer:Their will be a progress bar that will tell you how much of the print is done, and how much is left.
